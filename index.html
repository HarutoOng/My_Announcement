FOR ALL RESIDENTS AND HOMEOWNERS

Good day, neighbors and residents! In our continuous effort to maintain a safe, clean, and healthy environment, the Barangay Council warmly invites all community members to participate in our upcoming Barangay-Wide Clean-Up Drive and General Assembly.

Important Reminders:

During the community assembly, we will discuss new security protocols, neighborhood waste management programs, and the distribution of free calamity preparedness kits. Please remember to bring your own work gloves, masks, and a valid community ID or proof of residency for quicker verification at the registration desks.
